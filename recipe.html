Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

These golden-brown cookies are crisp on the edges, soft in the middle, and loaded with melty chocolate chips. A timeless favorite for kids and adults alike.

Recipe Information

  • Prep time: 15 mintues
  • Cook time: 12 mintues
  • Servings: 24 cookies
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.
  3. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake 10–12 minutes until golden brown.

Additional Information

  • Store in airtight container for 5 days.
  • Freeze dough balls for up to 3 months.

Nutrition (per cookie):

  • Calories: 150
  • Fat: 8g
  • Carbs: 20g
  • Protein: 2g

Vegetable Stir-Fry

A colorful medley of vegetables stir-fried in a light soy-ginger sauce. Quick, healthy, and versatile.

Recipe Information

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Servings: 4
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok.
  2. Stir-fry garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Stir in sauces and toss well.

Additional Information

  • Great with rice or noodles.

Nutrition (per serving):

  • Calories: 160
  • Fat: 7g
  • Carbs: 20g
  • Protein: 5g

Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes that make the perfect breakfast treat—enjoy with syrup, fruit, or butter.

Recipe Details

  • Prep time: 15 minutes
  • Cook time: 12 minutes
  • Servings: 12 pancakes
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and butter.
  3. Pour batter onto greased skillet.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook 1–2 minutes more.

Additional Information

  • Add blueberries or chocolate chips for variation.

Nutrition (per pancake):

  • Calories: 110
  • Fat: 3g
  • Carbs: 18g
  • Protein: 3g
